InsurBench Annotation Validator

The task

InsurBench is a reasoning-focused, yes/no QA dataset over insurance policy documents. Each item has been answered in advance. Your job is to check those answers against the policy and validate them.
  1. Open a document. You get the policy PDF on the left and, on the right, the question, answer, reasoning, the mentioned sections, and the reasoning type.
  2. Read the question and find the relevant passages in the PDF (use search and zoom).
  3. For the question, answer, reasoning, and reasoning type, mark Correct or Incorrect. When you mark something Incorrect, a box opens to rewrite it (or the alternative is offered); the original value can't be re-selected.
  4. For each mention, mark Correct if it is relevant and correctly written, otherwise Incorrect (it gets struck through). Add any missing section with the + button.
  5. Progress autosaves in your browser. Use Export JSON for one document, or Export all (merged) on this page to download everything at once.

What helps you

  1. PDF viewer: search text, zoom in and out, and scroll the policy on the left.
  2. Drag the divider between the two panes to resize them.
  3. Use the Zoom In / Zoom Out buttons on the right to fit the form to your screen.
  4. Each document card shows your validation progress.
