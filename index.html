InsurBench Annotation Validator

The task

InsurBench is a reasoning-focused, yes/no QA dataset over insurance policy documents. Each item below has been answered in advance. Your job is to check those answers against the policy and validate them.
  1. Open a document. You get the policy PDF, a yes/no question, the reasoning, the section names used, and a reasoning type.
  2. Read the question and find the relevant passages in the PDF (use search and zoom).
  3. For each field, mark Correct or Incorrect. If incorrect, pick or write the right value (the original value is blocked, so you can't re-select it).
  4. For mentions, tick the section names that are right; add or fix any in the box.
  5. Progress autosaves in your browser. Click Export JSON when done.

Reasoning types

EEC — Exclusion exception chain: cover applies, an exclusion removes it, then an exception may restore it (or none applies). The answer follows that chain to its end.
PB — Provision boundary: one clause sets a scope or limit, and the answer turns on whether the facts fall inside or outside it.
PI — Provision interaction: two or more clauses apply, and the outcome comes from their combined effect.
CE — Condition evaluation: a clause applies only if stated conditions are met; you check each against the facts.
